User-Stories

User-stories provide a simple, easy to read, articulation of what users of the tresplace app can do. They are the reference document for what needs to be included in the MVP (mininum viable product).

AS a Coffee Creator, I can ...

  • make a 'Pourtrait' of my coffee shop
  • upload high quality images to my shop pourtrait
  • upload high quality video to my shop pourtrait
  • uplaod an investigation* to my shop pourtrait
  • show investigation results infographically and textually
  • customize my investigation or choose a pre-packaged one
  • include questions and/or modules in my investigation
  • be seduced to fill out my pourtrait with sexy tresPlace UI
  • post my active menu of coffee drinks
  • post events
  • post promotional pricing

As Coffee Lovers we can ...

  • request that a coffee creator builds an Investigation
  • create my own investigation
  • 'like' many coffee shops
  • 'love' only one coffee shop
  • 'like' many coffee drinks
  • 'love' only one coffee drink
  • enable our own lover-pourtrait
  • show our relationship status to coffee drinks and shops
  • display the investigations we've filled out
  • share our investigation results through social media
  • read a lovers & creators - feed
  • see our relationship status and that of others
  • see our social media frends pourtraits
  • can write love letters to our fave coffee shops
