Branding & Building

This is the fleetCreature process for establishing the core brand and building an identity that will attract more resources

Below is the process broken out into weekly installations

Week 1: Starting Line

Preparations and Research

PURPOSE

  • Setup project foundation
  • Gather informal information about the general population already in the area

DELIVERABLES

  • Sign working contract
  • Setup workflow management system
  • Review this document
  • Conduct interviews with the public

Week 2: Research

Market & Historical Research

PURPOSE

  • Align branding effort with history Boulder and the building
  • Align branding effort with strategic market trends

DELIVERABLES

  • Historical research: library, Boulder Architecture Library, town records, interviewing old time-Boulderites
  • Review & research what went wrong with past businesses in the space
  • Research Boulder transportations behaviors: biking, walking, driving, bussing
  • Gather market data relevant to co-working spaces nationally and internationally

Week 3: Roundtable

Discussion with Community Stakeholders

PURPOSE

  • Engage target user-base in formative conversation
  • Determine key points of pain and pride among user-groups
  • Drill down into key stakeholder motivations and needs

DELIVERABLES

  • Interviews with key stakeholders (founders)
  • Hosted dicussion with key user-group representatives

Week's 4 & 5: Synthesize and Deliver

Compose and Present 1st Draft Deliverables

PURPOSE

  • Present research compilation
  • Message development strategy
  • Visual brand asset strategy
  • Settle on a name

DELIVERABLES

  • 1st Draft: Website splash page
  • 1st Draft: Pitch Decks
  • 1st Draft: Logo
  • 1st Draft: Tagline
  • Name
  • 1st Draft: Brand strategy document

Week 6: Presentation

Present Final Draft Deliverables

PURPOSE

  • Launch the Project
  • Integrate specific feedback

DELIVERABLES

  • Website splash page
  • Pitch Decks
  • Logo
  • Tagline
  • Brand strategy document

Roles and Accountabilities

Within fleetCreature

ROLES

  • Project Manager
  • UI / UX Designer
  • Branding Strategist

ACCOUNTABILITIES

  • Project Management: communication, workflow, project deliverables, primary point of contact
  • UI / UX Design: visual and creative design and leadership
  • Branding Strategy: branding process design and implementation

Notes

The projects resources will be maintained and managed through the Basecamp project management software, which we will discuss next. Also, we will rely on google plus and regular check-in meetings throughout the project.
