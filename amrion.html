b-chillin

Microsite Design & Code

Amrion is a nutraceutical company specializing in high quality natural supplements.

This bid covers design & coding of a microsite for the product B-Chillin. The site will act as landing page for the YouTube promotional video and will act as a portal for people to sign up for a free sample of the product & also learn more about the product & company.
