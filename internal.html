TRANSPARENCY No big secrets here. Only hard work & communication. We're all adults & deserve to be informed. We believe in transparency of finances & roles & thus all wages & work structure is public knowledge (just ask!)

OWNERSHIP We want leaders, not sheep. People who want to take ownership and have real accountability. From your first day as a contractor of fleetCreature you will start earning shares & after $50k in wages can begin taking part in shareholder profits & decision making.

AUTONOMY We are not a 9-5 company. We expect results but give you freedom in determining how you will make those results happen. We encourage you to take the lead, make decisions & solve probelms on your own. Leadership will not be frowned upon - ask forgiveness, not permission.
