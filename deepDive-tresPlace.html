tresPlace

Deep Dive with fleetCreature

WHAT A story-driven platform that connects coffee lovers with coffee shops.

WHY There’s an art to finding the perfect coffee shop--go beyond the search to truly connect.

WHO Coffee drinkers, coffee shop owners, and coffee vendors.

HOW Self-funded, revenue-generating MVP driven by an imaginative team of believers.

WHERE Boulder County > Portland > U.S. > World

Why

To Serve the Coffee Culture and Market Opportunity

Coffee has become a huge part of the American lifestyle ($12 billion in annual sales), and yet no platform currently exists that captures and encourages the emotionality behind this experience. This app allows coffee lovers to express themselves around their coffee experiences, discover their preferences, and explore this part of their world. The competition (Yelp, Facebook, Google Places) provides an overly reductive rating system that ignores the complexity and particularities that are so central to coffee lovers’ experiences. It also doesn’t allow for coffee shop owners to capture the soul of their stories and form personal connections with their patrons.

COMPETITION

  • Yelp - Single rating system, no way to group user feedback into a manageable, readable way
  • Google Places - Focuses primarily on location, single rating system
  • Facebook - Poor search function

ROLE MODELS

  • Air B&B - Nice design, difficult for users to tell a bad story
  • OKCupid - Our relationship to our coffee shop is similar to our romantic relationships

What

The platform for drinkers, vendors, & owners

A platform in which patrons, owners, and vendors create rich profiles to connect around shared values and stories. Establishments are rated across a detailed list of factors, from machinery used to products available to ambience and service style. Coffee shop profiles are built along “storylines,” fields which serve as prompts for key story elements (e.g. founder’s story, trials and tribulations, vision). user profiles serve as a way for coffee lovers to claim their idiosyncratic preferences, express their loyalties (and rivalries), and search for new coffee shops anywhere in the world based on their detailed preferences. These functionalities will allow coffee lovers to go beyond the search and connect with coffee shops that fit their unique style, similar to online dating platforms.

KEY FEATURES

  • Client/Vendor profiles as mediums for guided storytelling
  • User profiles as mediums for claiming idiosyncrasies, loyalties, rivalries
  • Story prompts
  • Content makes use of coffee words (e.g. grind, brew, pour, sip, etc.)
  • Structured data across detailed parameters
  • Subscription plans/membership
  • Free coffee vouchers for members
  • Promo codes/invite only
  • Rating system: cup half empty/full

Who

Drinkers, Vendors, & Owners

This product is geared towards coffee shop and tea house owners, patrons, and vendors looking to share their stories and make deep connections with one another. The product will help coffeeshop owners and vendors to brand themselves, identify points of customer loyalty, and bring more patrons through their doors. Likewise, it will allow users to claim their needs, wants, and loyalties, using this data to improve search functions.

VALUE OFFERED TO OWNERS & VENDORS

  • Get help telling your story; let your customers know why you’re special
  • Get more first-time customers through the door
  • Reconnect with original passion/motivation/reasoning
  • Develop greater customer loyalty

VALUE OFFERED TO COFFEE LOVERS

  • Express yourself fully; tell people about yourself
  • Discover something about yourself you didn’t already know
  • Explore your world
  • Find your coffee shop
  • Cultivate a sense of belonging
  • Free coffee!

How

Founder-Funded MVP

MVP product will be developed using the Build-Measure-Lean Feedback Loop. Initial funding will be provided by founders and supplemented later by angel/crowdsourced investments. MVP product will generate its own revenue through targeted ads and membership sales and will be launched in a major city and/or at a major event. Team will consist of primary founders and contract developers and designers. Advisory Board will be created upon launch of MVP and will consist of people from the tech and coffee industries.
