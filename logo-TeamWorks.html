Licoln Hills TeamWorks

Branding : Logo

Lincoln Hills TeamWorks is a program that offers youth ages 16-20 opportunities to gain work experience while introducing them to outdoor recreation and environmental stewardship. The program emphasizes respect for natural and cultural diversity. They encourage participant enthusiasm, dedication, strong work ethic and good team relationships. It is the hope of TeamWorks to offer an experience which helps our participants find happy and healthy professional and personal paths into adulthood.

This bid covers timeline & estimates for a logo design for the program for use on the web and in printed materials.
