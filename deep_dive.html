QUESTIONS

  • What are your core values?
  • Why are you making this product/ what do you hope to achieve with this product?
  • What exactly is the product?
  • Who are your target users and/or clients?
  • What characterizes your target users and/or clients?
  • What is the size of the target market (both clients & users)
  • What problem are you solving for your clients?
  • What are you offering that is novel to this niche?
  • Who is your main competition?
  • What makes your product more desireable to users/clients than you main competition?
  • What is you plan for long term devlopment of the app?
  • How will you execute this plan?
  • What is your profit model/models?
  • What kind of funding are you looking to procure in the future & when?
  • What are the emotions that will be associated with your brand?
  • What technological trends will your product take advantage of/ leverage?

PLAN FOR MVP

  • What is the budget for MVP?
  • Who are the MVP users/clients?
  • How will these users/clients be found?
  • What is the launch strategy for MVP?
  • What resources need to be procured with this budget?
  • What is the timeline?
  • What platforms & technologies will be used for MVP?
  • How well will these technologies scale?

CREATE USER STORIES

  • User stories are widely used in tech development and provide a blueprint to effectively plan & manage design & programming for the MVP production phase.
  • In this portion of the dive we will document all users stories & decide those which should go into MVP and those which should be put in the icebox for later based on timeline, budget & the target audience for MVP.

WIREFRAMING EXCERCISE

  • During the final portion of the MVP workshop we will facilitate a wireframing exercise in which company stakeholders pair with fleetCreature experts to sketch out ideas for the main views of the app.
  • This part of the processes will combine all of the information generated in the Deep Dive & start the processes of moving the app from idea to physical product.
