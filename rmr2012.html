Rocky Mtn Ruby 2012

website & branding

Rocky mountain ruby is a developers conference in September in Boulder, CO. It is known for it's fun interaction with the Boulder tech scene & the Boulder outdoors & for provision of great local eating & drinking experiences.

This bid covers branding for the conference for use on collateral & the website. It also includes the conference website that will be used to promote the conference, sell tickets, offer scheduling & important info during the conference & provide reflecetion/ sales for next year after the conference.
