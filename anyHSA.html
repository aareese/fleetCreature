any HSA

UI/UX & visual design for iOS app

anyHSA is a mobile app to help people manage thier HSA accounts and track thier health expenses & deductibles for the year.

This bid covers timeline & estimates for high level UI/UX design work on the app as well as visual design. The timeline has been created to take into account a shorter than average development cycle that will then be augmented by future iterations of the app.

Tapflow/Wireframes

Feb 13th-17th (17-23 hours)

PURPOSE

  • Review existing UI & wireframes & suggests improvements that will increase usability & simplicity.

DELIVERABLES

  • Tapflow & wireframes of main views 12-15 hours (Wed Feb 15th)
  • Completed wireframe set 5-7 hours (Fri Feb 17th)

Visual Design

Feb 20th-March 2nd (14-17 hours)

PURPOSE

  • Create visual skin & branding for the app.

DELIVERABLES

cost included
